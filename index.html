CENTRE FOR DATA SCIENCE AND ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
DATA SCIENCE

The last few years have witnessed an explosion in the quantity and variety of data available in Africa and around the globe. This data is produced either as a by product of digital services, from sensors or measuring devices, satellites and from many other sources. A number of practical fields have been transformed by the ability to collect and process large volumes of data from various sources. At DSAIL we are heavily involved in Data Science research and development of data processing tools that can convert large chunks of data to useful informtion which is in-turn used in decision making.

AI & MACHINE LEARNING

Artificial intelligence (AI) emphasises the creation of intelligent machines/systems that function like humans. AI has been applied to many real-world applications. Machine learning is a branch of AI based on the idea that systems can learn from data, identify hidden patterns, and make decisions with little/minimal human intervention. At DSAIL we are involved in Machine learning research and utilization of ready-made machine learning models (For Example - classification models) in data analysis.
